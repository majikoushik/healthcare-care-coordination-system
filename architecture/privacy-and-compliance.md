# Privacy And Compliance-Readiness

This project demonstrates compliance-readiness patterns. It does not claim HIPAA compliance, certification, or clinical approval.

## Policy

- Use synthetic demo data only.
- Do not log full clinical notes, secrets, tokens, full addresses, or sensitive health details.
- Audit events should store event facts and safe identifiers, not clinical note text.
- Clinical AI insights are informational demo output and require qualified healthcare professional review.

## Readiness Patterns

- Audit logging direction.
- Correlation ID traceability.
- RBAC role model readiness.
- Safe configuration and Key Vault direction.
- Responsible AI boundaries for clinical note insights.
